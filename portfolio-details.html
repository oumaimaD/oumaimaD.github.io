StrokeVision - Advanced Stroke Analysis

  • Category: Medical Imaging & AI
  • Field: Brain Stroke Detection & Segmentation
  • Project Date: November, 2023
  • Demo URL: Watch Online

Our Solution

StrokeVision is an end-to-end intelligent platform that revolutionizes stroke lesion detection and segmentation from MRI scans. Designed with both medical accuracy and computational efficiency in mind, it integrates advanced image preprocessing, a rich palette of segmentation techniques, and robust evaluation strategies.

Preprocessing & Filtering

  • Gaussian Blur – reduces Gaussian noise while preserving structural details.
  • Mean & Median Filters – smooth tissue regions and eliminate salt-and-pepper noise.
  • Motion Blur Kernel – simulates real patient motion and improves robustness.
  • Adaptive Canny Edge Detection – highlights critical brain boundaries with dynamic thresholds.

Segmentation Palette

  • Chan-Vese Active Contours – precise region-based segmentation.
  • K-Means Clustering – unsupervised intensity-based partitioning.
  • Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM) – probabilistic clustering for heterogeneous tissues.
  • U-Net Deep Learning Model – state-of-the-art segmentation with pixel-level accuracy.

Data Augmentation

To improve model generalization, StrokeVision applies extensive data augmentation techniques: rotations, horizontal/vertical flips, intensity shifts, random noise injection, and contrast variations. In addition, a GAN Pix2Pix model was trained to generate synthetic MRI scans, expanding the dataset with realistic variations and significantly boosting segmentation robustness.

Evaluation & Clinical Impact

Segmentation performance is rigorously validated with Adjusted Rand Index (ARI=0.928), Normalized Mutual Information (NMI=0.875), and Silhouette Score=0.850. The resulting system delivers:

  • Faster diagnostic support for neurologists.
  • Early detection of stroke lesions for timely intervention.
  • Improved patient outcomes through AI-assisted decision-making.

By bridging medical imaging expertise with artificial intelligence innovation, StrokeVision is a powerful demonstration of how AI can actively assist clinicians and transform healthcare.
